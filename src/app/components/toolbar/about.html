About ElderDrug

Elderdrug.com was created to make the AGS Beers Criteria easier to search. Published triennally since 2011, Beers Criteria provides a list of medications to be used with caution in adults 65+.

Citation

American Geriatrics Society 2019 Beers Criteria Update Expert Panel. American Geriatrics Society 2019 Updated AGS Beers Criteria for Potentially Inappropriate Medication Use in Older Adults. J Am Geriatr Soc. 2019; 67: 674-694
